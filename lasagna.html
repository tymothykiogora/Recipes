Ingredients
  • (8 pound) bone-in smoked ham
  • 2 fruit, (2-5/8" dia, sphere)s oranges, juiced
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon dry mustard
  • 1 tablespoon whole cloves, or as needed

Steps
  1. Step 1 Place ham in a large pot and cover with water. Soak 8 hours to overnight to remove salt.
  2. Step 2 Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Line a roasting pan with aluminum foil.
  3. Step 3 Mix orange juice, brown sugar, and dry mustard together in a small bowl.
  4. Step 4 Drain ham and pat dry. Place in the prepared roasting pan. Score the outside of the ham in a diagonal pattern and coat with orange juice mixture. Insert cloves at cross marks.
  5. step 5 Bake in the preheated oven until heated through and glaze is well browned, about 2 hours. Let sit for 20 to 30 minutes before carving.
